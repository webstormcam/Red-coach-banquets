WEDDINGS

At the Red Coach Inn, we help you plan and hold your Niagara Falls wedding in the setting you’ve dreamed of. Whether you’re looking for an intimate ceremony or a grand dinner reception, let our dedicated staff help you create a day to remember forever. We also plan vow renewals, dinner rehearsals, and anniversaries.

Traditional Reception Package:

It is an exciting time when you book your reception and with the Red Coach Inn you are guaranteed to receive a special touch in service. The Traditional Reception package includes the following:

  • Special room rates for overnight the wedding party and overnight guests (rates and availability are based upon season and occupancy)
  • Choice of Niagara Room or Riverview Room
  • Complimentary champagne toast for the Bride & Groom
  • Cake-cutting service with wares
  • Votive candles for guest tables
  • Favor placement
  • White table linens and napkins
  • White tulle and white lights for the gift and cake table (available upon request)
  • Menu tasting to perfect your culinary package
  • Vegetarian and special dietary options
